Field Research / Ergonomics

Handloom Weaver Ergonomic Study — Solapur

A field-based student research project investigating posture-related discomfort among local handloom weavers and identifying opportunities for ergonomic intervention.

My Role
  • Designed the field research framework
  • Guided observation and interview methods
  • Mentored insight synthesis and ergonomic opportunity mapping
Key Insight
Extended floor-level weaving posture, limited back support, and repetitive work patterns indicated a clear need for an affordable ergonomic seating intervention.
